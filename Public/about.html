My Story

SnapLocate began as a personal project at Thapar University to simplify how students access critical campus information. With real-time faculty listings, classroom locations, and academic tools, it bridges the gap between physical space and digital clarity.

My Mission

To transform how students interact with their campus-making it more user-friendly, accessible, and digitally connected.

Leadership

SnapLocate is led by a single independent developer, committed to continual innovation, open feedback, and meaningful impact in academic life.
